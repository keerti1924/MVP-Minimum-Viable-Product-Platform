Who We Are

Welcome to Eco Apparel Disposal

We are passionate about reducing textile waste and supporting those in need. Our platform connects users with trusted partners who specialize in sustainable disposal, donation, and recycling of apparel.
By facilitating easy drop-off or pick-up services, we help reduce landfill waste and promote responsible fashion consumption.

Our mission is to make clothing disposal eco-friendly, reduce textile waste, and ensure that no wearable clothing goes unused.
